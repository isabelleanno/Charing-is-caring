About Us

Chairing is Caring was founded by two interior designers in 2015. Everyone in our team knows a thing or two about design. Our goal is to make the world a more beautiful place with reasonably-priced yet high-quality interior design that benefits everyone. We support small furniture businesses that use ethically sourced materials, as well. All the money we make goes to the employees, or back to our organization to improve ourselves. We give 7% of our profits to Habitat for Humanity to build homes for the less fortunate. By supporting us, you support them, too. Everyone in the world needs to keep sharing.

Who we are

Jackie Lenferink

Jackie has been an interior designer for 10 years, but has been passionate about the art since she was a child. She specializes in mid-century modern design. Her ability to mix the old with the new is astounding! Her work truly stands out and creates a fresh bold interior.

Sophia Brooke

Where there's warm minimalism, there's our Sophia! We have been so grateful to have her with us. She started as an intern here, and we instantly knew she had to stay. Over the 7 years she's been here, Sophia has mastered the art of minimalism and will grace your home with a spacous, cozy interior.

Roseanne Jones

Roseanne has worked all across the world over the last 10 years, transforming spaces in Greece, Norway, Finland and more. Once she came to the UK, she loved it and has now been a proud member of our team for 5 years. Roseanne will add splashes of neat cozy scandinavian feel to any space.
